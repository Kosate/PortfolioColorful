Thailand Olympiad in Informatics

An annual competitive programming competition for secondary school students in Thailand.
Contest consists of the complicated algorithm, data structure, and programming skills to solve the task.

I'm a consecutive participant at Thailand Olympiad in Informatics competitions (TOI). It's competitive programming using data structure and algorithm knowledge to solve the task.

I participated TOI competition and received an award for 3 years. I got a bronze medal from 7th competition, a gold medal at 8th, and a silver medal at 9th, the last year I participated.

ACM ICPC

ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest, is an annual multi-tiered competitive programming competition among the universities of the world.

Style of competition is the same as TOI competition. But a lot harder and a different. Specify three students in the single team. Participants must be university students.

I've join ACM-ICPC Asia Bangkok Regional Contest 2 years. Dive to 46th ranking at 2014 and 21th ranking in 2015 from 90 teams around the Asia.
